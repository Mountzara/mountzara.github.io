About Me

Dr. Christopher Zaragoza Mabini is a minimally invasive gynecologic surgery (MIGS) fellow at Ascension Illinois St. Francis Hospital in Evanston, IL. With extensive experience in obstetrics and gynecology, he is committed to advancing women's health through both clinical practice and research.

Education

  • Fellow, Minimally Invasive Gynecologic Surgery, Ascension Illinois St. Francis Hospital, Evanston, IL (July 2023 - July 2025)
  • Residency, OBGYN, Ascension Illinois St. Alexius, Hoffman Estates, IL (July 2019 - June 2023)
  • Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine, Alabama College of Osteopathic Medicine, Dothan, AL (July 2014 — May 2019)
  • Masters in Adult Education with concentration in Curriculum Development and Instructional Design, Troy University, Montgomery, AL (Sept. 2016 — May 2017)
  • Postgraduate Certificate in Public Health, University of Liverpool, UK (Sept. 2009 - July 2010)
  • Bachelors of Science in Biological Sciences, University of California, Irvine (Sept. 2002 — Sept. 2007)
